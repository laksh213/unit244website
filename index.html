Lit Fest 2023

Posted on March 22, 2023

Organized by the English Literary Organization, Lit Fest 2023 encompasses a vibrant book fair and competitions in speech, music, and photography. This festival offers a unique platform for literary enthusiasts and artists to showcase their talents and engage in cultural exchange.

Praana 2023

Posted on March 5, 2023

Praana 2023, organized by the SSL of SLLC, was a remarkable team building program followed by an entertaining evening. The event fostered collaboration and camaraderie among participants through various engaging activities, creating a memorable and fun-filled experience for all attendees.

Posted on October 15, 2023

The Social Service League Cricket Premier League, organized by the SSL, was held on October 15, 2023. Avurudu 2023

Posted on April 19, 2023

Avurudu 2023 marked the Sinhala and Tamil New Year celebration at SLLC. Held on April 19, 2023, this event was a vibrant display of cultural traditions, fostering a sense of unity and joy among students and faculty. It was an occasion filled with traditional games, music, and festivities, celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka.

150th Year Celebrations at SLLC

Posted on December 22, 2023

The Sri Lanka Law College, with its rich history dating back to 1874 as Ceylon Law College, celebrated its 150th anniversary. This milestone event highlighted the college's long-standing tradition of legal education in Sri Lanka and its significant role in shaping the legal landscape.
